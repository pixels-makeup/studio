Policy

预约前沟通

为了更好地为你服务，需要你给我发一下你的素颜照片，包括脸和头发长度。如果有喜欢的妆发类型，也麻烦你发给我参考，这样我能更好地了解你的喜好并准备相应材料，避免浪费你的时间，也能在工作时做出更合适的妆发。

如果你太忙，也麻烦你大概跟我说一下你想要什么。因为我没办法猜到每个人的想法，所以如果没有提前沟通，我也不一定能做出你心目中想要的效果。

如需更改时间

  • 婚礼：至少提早一个月通知。
  • 妆造：至少提前一天通知。
  • 如因临时情况需要临时更改时间，我只能安排别的空档时间，或保留定金一年。

温馨提示

  1. 造型前请集中做好皮肤保湿，例如敷保湿面膜、敷爽肤水、去死皮，并尽量多做保湿，同时保证睡眠。
  2. 如果头发需要修剪或补染，请提前预约发型师做好。推荐可以剪一些层次，这样头发更轻盈，也更方便我做造型。
  3. 如果可以，麻烦请不要种睫毛和纹眉。我会为你贴睫毛与画眉毛，并根据你想要的妆效选择浓密或自然感睫毛。如果已经种睫毛或纹眉，妆感就无法改变。
  4. 造型前一晚请在睡觉前洗头。如果早上时间充足，也可以早上洗头。洗头后请吹干，一定要保持头发蓬松干爽。
  5. 造型当天麻烦穿一件好脱、舒适的服装，例如后面拉链连衣裙或前面扣子的衬衫，方便造型后在不影响妆发的情况下更换婚纱或拍摄服装。

妆效与皮肤状态说明

小提示：针对皱纹、眼袋、法令纹、凸嘴、泪沟等属于骨骼与肌肉结构的问题，化妆只能做视觉修饰与弱化，无法根本性改变。如有较高要求，建议提前考虑医美方式。

如果近期有进行医美疗程或频繁熬夜等，皮肤可能会处于较为干燥、敏感或不稳定的状态。若有淤青、泛红等情况，我会尽量帮你做修饰遮盖，因此底妆相对会比平时厚一些。后续可能出现卡粉、浮粉或不如平常服贴的状况，还请提前知悉与理解。

拍摄结束后

眼妆卸妆时，请用眼唇卸妆液倒进卸妆棉敷一下，再慢慢擦干净。剩下的部分请用卸妆油或卸妆膏清洁面部，最后用洗面奶把残余洗干净，避免因为清洁不干净导致皮肤过敏。倒梳过的头发需要用护发素敷一会儿，之后即可梳开。

其他详细细节我们都可以多沟通。如果我忘记回复，麻烦你提醒我一下就好。非常感谢！

造型当天准备

为了让妆发过程更顺利、避免中途打断服务而来不及出门，请你提前做好以下准备：

  • 请提前佩戴好美瞳。
  • 请先换好上半身衣服或全身衣服。若是拖地长裙，可先不用穿，避免妆发过程中不小心踩到裙摆。
  • 头发请务必完全吹干，不能有任何湿气。
  • 建议提前吃一点东西补充体力与能量。

妆发过程中尽量不要频繁走动、处理其他事情、抠脸、扭头玩手机或吃东西。这样才能更有效率地完成整体造型，也能让妆发效果更精致持久。

上门服务准备

上门服务需要准备好一张没有杂物的大桌子，以及顾客自己坐的椅子。我会自备一张椅子。

Before Your Appointment

To better prepare for your service, please send me bare-face photos that clearly show your face and hair length. If you have makeup or hair references you like, please send them as well. This helps me understand your preferences, prepare the right materials, save time, and create a look that suits you better.

If you are busy, please still briefly tell me what kind of look you want. I cannot guess every client's preferences, so without communication beforehand, I may not be able to create exactly what you have in mind.

Rescheduling

  • Wedding services: please notify me at least one month in advance.
  • Makeup and hair services: please notify me at least one day in advance.
  • For last-minute changes, I can only move the appointment to another available time slot or keep the deposit valid for one year.

Preparation Notes

  1. Please focus on skin hydration before your appointment, such as using hydrating masks, toner masks, gentle exfoliation, and extra moisturizing. Please also get enough sleep.
  2. If your hair needs trimming or color touch-up, please book your hairstylist ahead of time. Some layers are recommended because they make the hair lighter and easier to style.
  3. If possible, please avoid lash extensions and eyebrow tattooing. I will apply lashes and shape/fill your brows based on the look you want, from natural to fuller lashes. Existing lash extensions or tattooed brows can limit the final makeup effect.
  4. Please wash your hair the night before your appointment, or in the morning if you have enough time. After washing, fully blow-dry your hair and keep it fluffy and dry.
  5. On the service day, please wear comfortable clothing that is easy to remove, such as a dress with a back zipper or a button-up shirt. This helps you change into your wedding dress or photoshoot outfit without affecting your makeup and hair.

Makeup Results And Skin Condition

Fine lines, eye bags, nasolabial folds, protruding mouth structure, tear troughs, and similar concerns are related to bone and muscle structure. Makeup can visually soften and refine them, but it cannot fundamentally change them. If you have higher expectations for structural changes, please consider medical aesthetic treatments in advance.

If you recently had medical aesthetic treatments or have been frequently staying up late, your skin may be dry, sensitive, or unstable. If there is bruising, redness, or similar concerns, I will do my best to correct and cover them, but the base makeup may be thicker than usual. There may also be creasing, floating powder, or less adherence than normal, so please be aware in advance.

After The Shoot

When removing eye makeup, soak a cotton pad with eye and lip makeup remover, press it on the eye area, then gently wipe it clean. For the rest of the face, use cleansing oil or balm, then wash with facial cleanser to remove residue and avoid skin irritation from incomplete cleansing. For backcombed hair, apply conditioner for a while before gently brushing it out.

We can communicate more about any details. If I forget to reply, please feel free to remind me. Thank you very much!

Service Day Checklist

To keep the makeup and hair process smooth and avoid delays, please prepare the following before we start:

  • Please put in your colored contacts in advance.
  • Please change into your top or full outfit first. If it is a floor-length gown, you may wait to put it on to avoid stepping on the hem during styling.
  • Your hair must be fully blow-dried with no moisture left.
  • I recommend eating something beforehand to keep your energy up.

During the service, please try not to walk around frequently, handle other tasks, touch your face, turn your head to use your phone, or eat. This helps me work more efficiently and helps the final look stay more refined and long-lasting.

On-Site Service Setup

For on-site service, please prepare a large clutter-free table and a chair for yourself. I will bring one chair for myself.
